The Rising Artists from the

Queen City of the South

Dive into Cebu City's musical tapestry, where local bands weave a rich sonic fabric. From indie soul to electrifying rock, each note resonates with the Queen City of the South's cultural heartbeat. These storytellers capture the essence of Cebu, creating a dynamic and diverse musical landscape that reflects the city's spirit and vitality.

What is

Banda-rito?

Banda-Rito is Cebu's musical revelation, dedicated to propelling local talents into the limelight. With a mission to make these rising artists household names, the platform strives to bring their craft to the masses. By cultivating widespread recognition, Banda-Rito not only boosts bands' artistic growth but also fosters a supportive community, ensuring that each musical journey from Cebu resonates far beyond, creating a lasting impact on the region's cultural and creative landscape.

HNT

(Hang in There)

A pop/indie band of three from Cebu. Formed in summer 2018. HNT members decided to create music based on a shared belief of hope and inspiration for their would-be listeners. Influenced by the popular phrase 'Hang in There', together they are HNT with their vocalist Justine Chloe, bassist John Emmanuel and Nicolas Brent on drums.

NOMER

Introducing NOMER, a band that weaves a captivating tapestry of emotions through their music. With a unique blend of inspiration, heartbreak, and romance, NOMER delivers soul-stirring melodies and poignant lyrics that resonate deeply. Immerse yourself in their evocative sound and experience a journey through the myriad facets of the human heart.

GIRLKISSERS

A group of old souls inspired by the new wave of indie retro pop. Enjoy lip-smacking tunes and pure delight, the Girl kissers will make it right!
